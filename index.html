My Goal

What do I strive for?

One trait that I value the most in good design is prioritizing the user’s experience and consideration above all else. Whether it's a fun, creative, and innovative design, it's always crucial to consider if a user would find everything to be optimal.

At the end of the day, I design because one of my goals is to pursue a career that can positively change people’s lives. With UX/UI design, the field is closely connected to how the user interacts with the product. I know my designs will have a direct impact on how people feel, and my ultimate aim is to make things easier for them.
